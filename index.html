I am currently a Senior Researcher at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National Technical University of Athens, advised by Prof. Konsantinos Tserpes. I received a BSc Degree in Computer Science in 2013 and an MSc Degree in Web Engineering in 2015, both from Harokopio University of Athens. In 2022, I completed my PhD in the area of Distributed Systems at the same department.

I am currently a member of the Efficient Computing Lab, specializing in Efficient Computing and Data Analytics. My main research interests include Distributed Computing, Edge and Cloud Computing, Big Data Management and Analysis, Machine/Deep learning, NoSQL Database System, High Performance Computing (HPC), Spatiotemporal and Trajectory Analysis and Stream Processing.

I have participated in numerous EU funded and national projects.

Past Projects

  • CHARITY (H2020)

    CHARITY aspires to leverage the benefits of intelligent, autonomous orchestration of cloud, edge, and network resources, to create a symbiotic relationship between low and high latency infrastructures that will facilitate the needs of emerging applications.

    Jan 2021 – Jul 2024

  • SmartShip (H2020-MSCA-RISE-2018)

    A data analytics, decision support and circular economy – based multi-layer optimization platform towards a holistic energy efficiency, fuel consumption and emissions management of vessels.

    May 2019 – Mar 2024

  • FNS-Cloud (H2020)

    Food Nutrition Security Cloud (FNS-Cloud) has launched the first ‘food cloud’, federating existing and emerging datasets, tools and services to support European research.

    Apr 2019 – June 2023

  • GLASSEAS (National funded ESPA 2019)

    A global, distributed surveillance system for early detection and analysis of anomalous vessel trajectories.

    Apr 2019 – June 2020

  • MASTER (H2020-MSCA-RISE-2017)

    MASTER (Multiple ASpects TrajEctoRy management and analysis) is a project with the objective of forming an international and inter-sectoral network of organisations working on a joint research programme to define new methods to build, manage and analyse multiple aspects semantic trajectories.

    Mar 2018 – Dec 2023
